An Early Warning Sign of Critical Transition in the Antarctic Ice Sheet: A Data-Driven Tool for a Spatiotemporal Tipping Point

Nonlinear Processes in Geophysics, 28(1):153–166 2021 Open Access

Authors: A. R. AlMomani and Erik Bollt

Develops a data-driven early warning system for detecting critical transitions (tipping points) in the Antarctic marine ice sheet using satellite remote sensing data. The method identifies spatiotemporal precursors to irreversible ice loss without requiring a physics-based climate model.
