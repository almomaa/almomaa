Generalizing Geometric Partition Entropy for the Estimation of Mutual Information in the Presence of Informative Outliers

Chaos, 35(3):033141 2025 Open Access

Authors: C. Tyler Diggans and A. R. AlMomani

This paper extends the Geometric Partition Entropy framework to estimate mutual information in settings where informative outliers are present. Traditional estimators either fail in the presence of outliers or aggressively remove them, discarding potentially critical signal. The generalized GPE approach preserves outlier information while maintaining robust estimation.
