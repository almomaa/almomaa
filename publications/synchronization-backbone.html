The Essential Synchronization Backbone Problem

Chaos, 31(11) 2021 Open Access

Authors: C. T. Diggans, J. Fish, A. R. AlMomani, and E. Bollt

Poses and addresses the essential synchronization backbone problem: given a complex network that synchronizes, what is the minimal subnetwork that still supports synchronization? Practical implications for power grid stability, communication networks, and neural circuits.
